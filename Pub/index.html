📚 HTTP协议学习要点

HTTP请求格式:

GET /index.html HTTP/1.1 Host: localhost:8080 User-Agent: Mozilla/5.0 Accept: text/html [请求体]

HTTP响应格式:

HTTP/1.1 200 OK Content-Type: text/html Content-Length: 1234 Connection: close [响应体]

常见状态码:

  • 200 OK: 请求成功
  • 404 Not Found: 资源不存在
  • 405 Method Not Allowed: 方法不被允许
  • 500 Internal Server Error: 服务器内部错误
